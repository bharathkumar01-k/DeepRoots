SliceRooms

Introducing SliceRooms, a groundbreaking micro stay concept online travel agency (OTA). Since its inception in 2015, SliceRooms has rapidly expanded its presence in the Indian market, successfully partnering with over 1300 hotels across 100+ cities. As pioneers in this niche sector, we have established a strong foothold in all frontiers of the industry. With our user-friendly platform and extensive network, travellers can now easily book short stays at affordable rates, revolutionizing the way people experience hospitality on their journeys.

Our platform offers an innovative solution to address the problem of underutilized rooms, allowing hotels to maximize their revenue potential while providing customers with flexible and affordable accommodation options. With SliceRooms.com, travellers can now book rooms for shorter durations, catering to their specific needs without having to pay for an entire day's stay.

By leveraging our cutting-edge technology and extensive network of partner hotels, we ensure that travellers have access to a wide range of options at competitive prices. Our user-friendly interface and secure booking process guarantee a seamless experience for both hoteliers and guests.

Join us in reshaping the way people travel and make the most out of unutilized room inventory. Experience convenience, affordability, and flexibility like never before with SliceRooms.com.
