Located in the picturesque ECR (East Coast Road), Orchid Resort is a charming hotel that offers a total of 32 well-appointed rooms. Situated amidst serene surroundings, this resort provides an ideal getaway for both leisure and business travellers.

One of the standout features of Orchid Resort is its expansive one lac square feet open banquet area, which serves as a versatile space for hosting various events and celebrations. Whether it's a wedding reception, corporate gathering, or any other special occasion, this banquet area can be tailored to meet the specific needs and preferences of guests.

In addition to the banquet area, Orchid Resort also boasts a conference hall that is equipped with modern amenities. This makes it an excellent choice for hosting conferences, seminars, workshops, and other professional events.

After a long day of work or exploration in ECR's scenic attractions, guests can indulge in delightful culinary experiences at the resort's restaurant. With its diverse menu offering both local and international delicacies, there is something to satisfy every palate.

For those seeking relaxation and unwinding after a busy day, Orchid Resort also features a bar where guests can enjoy refreshing beverages along with light snacks. The bar provides a cosy ambiance where one can unwind with friends or colleagues. Overall, Orchid Resort offers an all-encompassing experience for guests looking for comfortable accommodation paired with exceptional event spaces and dining options. Its strategic location on the East Coast Road ensures easy accessibility to nearby attractions while providing tranquillity away from bustling city life.
