Deep Roots, expanding,Pondicherry, tourist location, south India, Puri, Orissa, Assam, Darzeeling

Deep Roots is thrilled to announce its upcoming expansion into three remarkable tourist destinations in India. The company has set its sights on Pondicherry in South India, Puri in the state of Orissa and the enchanting North Eastern state of Assam. Additionally, Deep Roots plans to establish a presence in the scenic town of Darjeeling.

These locations have been carefully selected due to their rich cultural heritage and growing popularity among tourists. Deep Roots aims to offer travellers an unforgettable experience by providing top-notch services and accommodation options that cater to their diverse needs.

By expanding into these vibrant destinations, Deep Roots intends to tap into the immense potential for tourism growth in these regions. With their team of experienced professionals and deep understanding of the local culture and attractions, Deep Roots is poised to become a key player in enhancing the tourism industry in Pondicherry, Puri, Assam and Darjeeling.

Stay tuned for further updates from Deep Roots as they embark on this exciting journey of expansion into some of India's most captivating travel destinations.
